Performing under Tikoda for at Green Works, Bristol. Designing work especially for an ultra-wide screen enabled thinking laterally. Photographed and filmed by Megan X Shoots.

For its launch event in December 2022, Theo Passingham was chosen to orchestrate the visual elements of the evening, showcasing acts like The Breath, Paul Nolan, Ishmael Ensemble, and Loss >< Gain.
