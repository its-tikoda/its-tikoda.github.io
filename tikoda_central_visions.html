Having been spotted performing my live AV set at Shambala Festival 2023 performing for Compass Presents, Seb got in touch about doing a show at some point in Bristol, which eventually led to their event Central Visions at Green Works. A great line-up of DJs, VJs and live electronic artists including Movement 81, Phaeleh, Caveman and more.

The highlights were the epic projector screen being placed in quite a free-party-esque setting with a suitably big rig adding to the sonics and aesthetics. It is always great to work with grassroots promotors and venues to create unique experiences.

I was very lucky to have Megan X Shoots on side to take some wonderful footage as well.
