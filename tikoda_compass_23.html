Having built up a relationship with Compass Presents over the years by performing there several times with Young Waters, I have been very blessed to have performed twice as Tikoda, once in 2022 and once in 2023.

This year they set up a back-projected screen which looked great, and their sound system was absolutely on-point. I played for a full hour and a half and it still felt short, it was such a great energy in there.

Thanks Emilie for the photos, and A.Whales for the hands.
